OUR INTRODUCTIONS

Kogi state Information Technology Development Agency (kictda)

To bridge the digital divide and for Kogi state to effectively join the emerging national economy necessitated the creation of Kogi state Information Technology Development Agency (kictda). The State government approved the policy through executive order in June 2022.

Hon. Kingsley Femi Fanwo
